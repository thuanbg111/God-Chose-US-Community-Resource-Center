About God Chose Us Community Resource Center

God Chose Us Community Resource Center is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law for charitable purposes. We operate as a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit with EIN 85-0897041.

Specific purpose: to provide assistance to those in need and to serve as a referral center for community resources.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
