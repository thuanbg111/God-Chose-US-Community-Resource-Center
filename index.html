God Chose Us Community Resource Center

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our specific purpose is to provide assistance to those in need and to serve as a referral center for community resources, connecting people with food, housing, healthcare, and social services.

What We Do

Direct help and trusted referrals for neighbors in crisis.

  • Emergency essentials: groceries & hygiene kits
  • Resource navigation: benefits, housing, and clinics
  • Outreach and community partnerships

Resource Navigation

One‑on‑one guidance and referrals to community services tailored to each family.

Emergency Support

Groceries, hygiene items, and critical supplies for those facing hardship.

Outreach & Partners

Events and collaborations that strengthen the safety net in Rialto.
